TotalEarn is freedom of choice. Holders can choose their rewards on a list of 41 different cryptocurrencies, among the most promising, or established ones on the market. TotalEarn could ease the task, creating a functional ecosystem where holders can compare the various coins' identities, before making their choice.

Our guidelines

We believe that a wide range of choices could be of great help for the holder's future investment. Giving the investor the chance of checking the actual potential of so many different projects, with no effort or financial burden, is what our project aims to, being the foundation of your investment portfolio. The list of rewarded coins will grow over time, as long as new, interesting project, will be launched on the blockchain.
